To develop an application capable of generating **Oscar-quality audio-visual (AV) content**, it must meet the stringent technical and artistic standards set by the **Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences (AMPAS)**. Below are the essential features required:

---

### **1. Video Quality Standards**  
#### **Resolution & Frame Rate**  
- **Minimum:** 2K (2048 Ã— 1080)  
- **Recommended:** 4K UHD (4096 Ã— 2160)  
- **Frame Rate:**  
  - **24 fps** (standard for live-action films)  
  - **48 fps** (allowed for high-frame-rate films, e.g., *The Hobbit*)  

#### **Color & Dynamic Range**  
- **Color Space:**  
  - **DCI-P3** (Digital Cinema Initiatives standard)  
  - **Rec. 2020** (for HDR content)  
- **Bit Depth:** **10-bit or higher** (for better color grading)  
- **HDR Support:** Dolby Vision, HDR10+  

#### **Codec & Compression**  
- **Master Format:**  
  - **Apple ProRes 4444 XQ** (for editing)  
  - **DNxHR 444** (Avid workflows)  
- **Delivery Format:**  
  - **DCP (Digital Cinema Package)** compliant with **SMPTE DCP standards**  

---

### **2. Audio Quality Standards**  
#### **Sound Format & Channels**  
- **Minimum:** 5.1 surround sound  
- **Recommended:** 7.1, Dolby Atmos, or IMAX 12-track  
- **Bit Depth & Sample Rate:**  
  - **24-bit / 48kHz** (minimum)  
  - **96kHz** (recommended for high-end productions)  

#### **Loudness & Dynamic Range**  
- **Compliance with ITU-R BS.1770-4** (LUFS standards)  
  - **Target Loudness:** -27 LUFS (for streaming)  
  - **Peak Level:** -2 dBTP (True Peak)  

#### **Audio Codecs**  
- **Uncompressed PCM** (WAV/AIFF)  
- **Dolby Digital (AC-3)** for theatrical DCP  

---

### **3. Editing & Post-Production Features**  
#### **Non-Linear Editing (NLE) Support**  
- **Frame-accurate cutting** (sub-frame audio editing)  
- **Multi-cam editing** (for complex scenes)  

#### **Color Grading Tools**  
- **Primary & Secondary Grading** (lift/gamma/gain, curves, hue vs. saturation)  
- **Support for LUTs (Look-Up Tables)**  
- **HDR Grading** (PQ/HLG curves)  

#### **Visual Effects (VFX) & Compositing**  
- **Node-based compositing** (e.g., Fusion, Nuke)  
- **Green screen keying** (Ultra Key, Primatte)  
- **3D tracking** (for CGI integration)  

---

### **4. Quality Control (QC) & Compliance Checks**  
#### **Automated QC Tools**  
- **Video:**  
  - **Black level check** (IRE 0-255)  
  - **Aspect ratio validation** (2.39:1, 1.85:1, etc.)  
- **Audio:**  
  - **Phase correlation analyzer** (for stereo issues)  
  - **Dialogue normalization** (for intelligibility)  

#### **DCP (Digital Cinema Package) Validation**  
- **Check for:**  
  - **Interop vs. SMPTE compliance**  
  - **Encryption status** (must be unencrypted for Oscars)
